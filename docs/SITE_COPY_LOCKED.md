## ABOUT PAGE (/about)

### Hero
**H1:** About

**Lead:** We're entering an era where AI stops being a tool and starts being a teammate. Not automation that replaces humans — systems where humans and AI agents work together, each doing what they do best. I call this the agent era, and I've spent my career building the infrastructure that makes these partnerships possible.

### Bio (3 paragraphs)

**P1:** Right now I'm at Machinify, building the platform infrastructure for an AI-native healthcare payments company processing billions in claims. My job is unifying five acquired platforms into one coherent system — the kind of complex, high-stakes, deeply technical problem I love. We're not just adding AI features to existing products. We're building systems where humans and AI agents work together as teammates — with agents that get assigned work, take action, and learn from outcomes.

**P2:** I spent 12 years writing code before moving into product. That background shapes everything about how I work — I think in systems, I speak fluent engineer, and I know the difference between architecture that scales and architecture that becomes technical debt. The pattern across my career: I build the infrastructure layer that makes everything else possible. Before Machinify, I led platform at League, pioneering AI-native healthcare platform architecture. Before that, I drove digital transformation at Nutrien, where we took a platform from $200M to $1.7B in 24 months — during a pandemic. Earlier chapters include leading product and engineering at Sikka Software, transforming an analytics provider into an AI-powered healthcare SaaS platform. And before product entirely, I was an engineer — semiconductor work, database systems, the foundational stuff.

**P3:** What I care about: Shipping on time with quality. Building for tomorrow while fixing today. Sharing knowledge freely. Good ideas come from everywhere — not just the loudest person in the room. Background: Stanford CS (joint graduate degree with ESIEE Paris). Santa Clara MBA. Based between California and Pennsylvania.
